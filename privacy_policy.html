Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 04, 2025

DW Innovation Pvt. Ltd. (“DW Innovation”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) is committed to respecting your privacy and protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal data in compliance with applicable data protection and privacy laws.

1. Objective

This Privacy Policy provides an overview of our privacy practices regarding personal information that we collect and process through our websites, services, applications, and other interactions with you. Our goal is to ensure transparency, accountability, and security in how we handle your data.

2. Information We Collect

  • Website & Device Data: IP address, browser type, device information, operating system, usage statistics, cookies, and tracking technologies.
  • Contact & Identity Information: Name, job title, email address, phone number, company details, address, LinkedIn/other professional profiles.
  • Account & Login Data: Username, password, authentication details (if you register for our services).
  • Communications & Feedback: Inquiries, comments, feedback, or correspondence you share with us.
  • Marketing Preferences: Subscription details, interests, opt-in/opt-out preferences.
  • Event/Media Information: Photos, recordings, or participation details in events, webinars, or workshops we organize.
  • Business & Contractual Data: Data provided by clients, vendors, or partners for contractual and service delivery purposes.

3. How We Collect Information

  • Directly from you (website forms, email, phone, events, or business communications).
  • Through our websites and applications (cookies, analytics, and telemetry).
  • From third-party partners, vendors, or affiliates.
  • Publicly available sources (social media, business directories, professional platforms).

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our software development and digital services.
  • Respond to your inquiries, support requests, or business communications.
  • Manage contracts, accounts, and business relationships.
  • Personalize user experience and deliver relevant content.
  • Send updates, newsletters, or marketing communications (with your consent).
  • Conduct analytics, research, and product development.
  • Ensure IT security, fraud prevention, and compliance with regulations.
  • Organize and manage events, workshops, and promotional campaigns.
  • Fulfill legal obligations, protect our rights, and resolve disputes.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contractual necessity – when processing is required to provide services you request.
  • Legitimate interest – for improving services, ensuring security, and business operations.
  • Consent – when you explicitly agree (e.g., marketing emails, event participation).
  • Legal obligation – when required by law, regulation, or government authorities.

6. Data Sharing & Transfers

We do not sell personal data. We may share your information with:

  • Subsidiaries, affiliates, and authorized employees.
  • Business partners and service providers (cloud, IT, analytics, or marketing vendors).
  • Professional advisors (auditors, consultants, legal counsel).
  • Government authorities or regulators, when legally required.

If data is transferred internationally, we ensure compliance with applicable data protection regulations and implement appropriate safeguards.

7. Data Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. Measures include encryption, access controls, secure servers, and regular audits.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law. Once no longer needed, data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

9. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or update your personal information.
  • Request deletion (“right to be forgotten”).
  • Restrict or object to data processing.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications.
  • Request data portability (transfer of your data to another provider).
  • Lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@dwinno.com.

10. Cookies & Tracking

Our websites may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance functionality, analyze usage, and deliver personalized experiences. You may control cookie settings through your browser. For details, see our Cookie Policy.

11. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If such data is identified, we will delete it promptly.

12. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies.

13. Updates to This Policy

DW Innovation may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in legal, technical, or business requirements. Updates will be posted on our website with a revised “Last Updated” date.
