AI & Machine Learning Intern

Join our team as an AI & Machine Learning Intern to explore cutting-edge technologies, AI agents, and emerging AI concepts while contributing to real-world intelligent system development.

Responsibilities

  • Assist in designing, developing, and testing AI/ML models
  • Research and implement advanced AI technologies and architectures
  • Explore and experiment with AI Agents (e.g., autonomous agents, LLM-powered agents)
  • Analyze datasets and build data pipelines for model training and evaluation

Requirements

  • 0-1 years of experience
  • Strong foundation in Python and ML libraries like TensorFlow, PyTorch, Scikit-learn, etc.
  • Familiarity with AI Agents (e.g., LangChain, AutoGPT, AgentGPT, BabyAGI) and their use cases
  • Understanding of NLP, Computer Vision, or Reinforcement Learning is a plus
  • Ability to work with datasets, data preprocessing, and model evaluation
  • Pursuing or recently completed a Bachelor’s/Master’s in Computer Science, AI, Data Science, or related field
